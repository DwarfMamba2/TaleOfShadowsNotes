The eastern-most anchor of the Loháven colonisation efforts. A port city of roughly 30k people of a wide variety of ancestries.

# Points of Interest
## <mark style="background: #FFB86CA6;">Infrastructure</mark>
### The Vigilant Watchtower
A towering structure overlooking the city, built as a defense against potential threats. Adventurers can climb to the top for a breathtaking view of Farshore Port and the surrounding landscapes.

### Church of Iomedae and Abadar
The largest church in town is a merged church between Iomedae and Abadar, the gods of justice and civilization. It rises as two great towers with a bridge in between, both adorned by beautiful glass art. Despite its height, the church leaves no shadow. ![[Pasted image 20230518201346.jpg]]
The bridge between the towers holds a large clock in the style of a golden sun. This hall is dedicated to [[Anor]], the force of Light. Here, [[The New Dawn]] has secret meetings. 
### Church of Erastil
A large but simple church, dedicated to followers of Erastil. 

### Church of Sarenrae
A round church, with a large domed roof clad in vines and healing herbs.


## <mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;">Shopping</mark>
### The Meridian Emporium #Questpoint 
A grand marketplace filled with stalls selling exotic goods from distant lands. Adventurers can find unique magical items, rare artifacts, and curious trinkets.

### The Arcane Attic
A mysterious shop run by a reclusive wizard. The store is filled with shelves stacked high with spellbooks, enchanted scrolls, and magical oddities. Knowledgeable adventurers might find a hidden gem here.

### The Silver Serpent #Questpoint 
A reputable blacksmith renowned for crafting exceptional weapons and armor. The owner, a grizzled dwarf, can also offer tips on local monster sightings and point the party towards hidden treasures. The owner has supposedly once seen a Sovereign Dragon streaking across the sky, he felt so inspired by its majesty that he named his shop in its likeness. In fact, he says he saw a similar skyserpant only

#### Quests #Quest
###### Goblinhunt
The blacksmith has received reports of a group of goblins terrorizing local travelers on a nearby road. The party is tasked with eliminating the goblin threat and retrieving his stolen goods. The blacksmith offers a reward of a custom-made weapon or armor piece.

##### Sunstone ore, undead mine
The blacksmith requires a rare metal called Sunstone Ore to complete a special commission. The party is tasked with venturing into an abandoned mine infested with undead creatures to retrieve the valuable ore. The undead creatures are harbooring in the mine to avoid the sun, and got stuck there after the last moon-pass.

### The Pioneers' Guild #Questpoint
An organization that celebrates and supports the pioneers who settled in Farshore Port. The guild offers resources, guidance, and quests related to exploring the uncharted lands beyond the city.


## <mark style="background: #FFF3A3A6;">Taverns</mark>
### The Dragon's Den Tavern
A lively establishment known for its dragon-themed decor and entertainment. Patrons can enjoy bards performing epic tales, while sipping on the famous Dragon's Breath Ale.
### The Seafarer's Rest
A cozy inn located near the harbor, frequented by sailors and adventurers alike. The walls are adorned with nautical memorabilia, and the Dwarf innkeeper serves the best seafood chowder in town.

# Story
## The Patron
[[The Patron]] har en villa i staden. Han vill att de ska investigatea [[The New Dawn]] så gott de kan, men om de har andra förslag på aktivitet så är han öppen.

## Investigate [[The New Dawn]]
De rekryterar från [[Farshore Port#Church of Iomedae and Abadar|Kyrkans]] mest aktiva och dedikerade följare, men även från stadens mest utsatta. De lovar dem en ny framtid där ljuset fyller alla hörn, och tills dess får de ta del av kyrkans bo och husrum. Prästen som leder deras rörelse är [[Farshore Port#Caleb Caustic]]. 

[[Juliana]] och hennes gäng kommer anlända i staden, och rapportera till [[Farshore Port#The Vigilant Watchtower]]. Efter de vart i staden i 5 dagar kommer de att bege sig till [[Shallowport]].
